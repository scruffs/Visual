About the author

Dave English was awarded his PhD in semiconductor Physics in 2012. What began as a desire to use computational techniques to assist with experiments, has blossomed into a full blown passion for machine learning and data-driven visualizations. The content of this site is an expression of that passion, and also a wish to share a little of the experiences gained traveling the world in the pursuit of science.

After spending a couple of years in Boston, he also developed an obsession with America’s Game, the NFL. So this site will contain details of the 2 things Dave knows; electrons and football.
